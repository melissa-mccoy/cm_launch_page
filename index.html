Common Ailments
Cough, Cold, Flu & Allergies

Upper respiratory infections are the broad category for flu, sinus infections, bronchitis, runny noses, coughs and colds. These common conditions can often be diagnosed through ConnectMed, keeping you out of the doctorâ€™s office and away from unnecessary germs.

Sore Throat

A sore throat is a very common symptom which could be an indicator of a series of ailments, including many that can often be treated via our video consultations. It can be difficult for a patient to know if they have a Cold, Allergies, Strep Throat, or Tonsillitis unless they visit with a doctor. Our doctors are trained to evaluate your symptoms and, depending on the clinical circumstances, diagnose and treat the various causes of a sore throat specifically through a video visit.

Urinary Tract Infections

Urinary tract infections (UTIs) are a very common condition. In fact, more often than not, when patients come to a doctor they have already experienced the symptoms and have been diagnosed with this condition before. While physicians will sometimes order a urinalysis, in many cases a urinalysis is not necessary to make a diagnosis. Either way, the physician may want to begin treatment before the urinalysis results come back. This is because a urine culture can take 48 hours before it starts growing any bacteria.

Minor Injuries

Sports and exercise activities are an important part of any healthy lifestyle. But with increased activity comes the increased risk of injury. In the absence of an obviously severe injury like a broken bone, most doctorâ€™s visits concerning sports injuries are patients looking for reassurance that the injury is not more severe. Sports injuries often fall into either a minor category (ankle sprains, wrist sprains, shoulder or rib soreness) or a severe category (broken bone, severely torn ligament/tendon).

Skin Conditions

Common conditions include rashes, STDs, poison ivy, insect bites, cold sores, psoriasis, bites, contact dermatitis, scrapes, allergies, hives, sores and boils.These conditions are most often diagnosable by visual inspection. An important component of dermatological assessment is recognizing patterns, features, signs and symptoms. Using your computer camera through our platform, you can upload multiple photos safely and directly to your physician who can also store them to assess changes over time. The photos are transmitted with lossless compression giving your doctor a precise view of the tissue.

Diarrhea & Vomiting

Diagnosing the cause and determining appropriate treatment of diarrhea typically begins with very focused clinical questions. Your physician may recommend a special diet, over the counter and/or prescription medications, or follow-up care. Our doctor are often able to determine the cause and provide treatment for vomiting during a video consultation by reviewing your medical history, asking questions, and performing an exam.

Mission

We seek to enable all Africans to live healthier, longer, and happier lives - no matter their location or income level.
Our strategy is to first target middle & upper income groups to generate the traction and revenue needed to then serve lower income populations.

Business Potential

We are a socially-minded for-profit company headquartered in South Africa with huge business potential. The social mindedness of our company doesn't detract from only strengthens the large financial potential of our company.


Market Size

Starting in South Africa

We will initially target middle & upper income populations (LSM 7-10) within South Africa and our market research shows that over 60% of this population is interested in using telemedicine. On-the-ground experience along with discussions with government and NGOs will inform our strategy for reaching lower income populations which will be planned slowly & carefully.


Expansion to the Continent

Expansion to other African countries with substantial LSM 7-10 populations like Nigeria, Kenya, and Ghana will naturally follow and you can see the market potential for the continent is over $6 billion. Given South Africa has one of the most well-respected medical systems on the continent, adoption here will mean smooth adoption elsewhere.


Monetization Strategy


While we see our data and machine learning tools being a large revenue generator, we've done revenue & cost projections focusing solely on our doctor video consultations which alone will allow us to break-even in the first year and reach over $11 million USD in profit by Year 5. The consultation price and doctor payment fee were decided upon through customer & doctor surveys and interviews.
