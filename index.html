What We Treat

Common Ailments
Cough, Cold, Flu & Allergies

Upper respiratory infections are the broad category for flu, sinus infections, bronchitis, runny noses, coughs and colds. These common conditions can often be diagnosed through ConnectMed, keeping you out of the doctor’s office and away from unnecessary germs.

Sore Throat

A sore throat is a very common symptom which could be an indicator of a series of ailments, including many that can often be treated via our video consultations. It can be difficult for a patient to know if they have a Cold, Allergies, Strep Throat, or Tonsillitis unless they visit with a doctor. Our doctors are trained to evaluate your symptoms and, depending on the clinical circumstances, diagnose and treat the various causes of a sore throat specifically through a video visit.

Urinary Tract Infections

Urinary tract infections (UTIs) are a very common condition. In fact, more often than not, when patients come to a doctor they have already experienced the symptoms and have been diagnosed with this condition before. While physicians will sometimes order a urinalysis, in many cases a urinalysis is not necessary to make a diagnosis. Either way, the physician may want to begin treatment before the urinalysis results come back. This is because a urine culture can take 48 hours before it starts growing any bacteria.

Minor Injuries

Sports and exercise activities are an important part of any healthy lifestyle. But with increased activity comes the increased risk of injury. In the absence of an obviously severe injury like a broken bone, most doctor’s visits concerning sports injuries are patients looking for reassurance that the injury is not more severe. Sports injuries often fall into either a minor category (ankle sprains, wrist sprains, shoulder or rib soreness) or a severe category (broken bone, severely torn ligament/tendon).

Skin Conditions

Common conditions include rashes, STDs, poison ivy, insect bites, cold sores, psoriasis, bites, contact dermatitis, scrapes, allergies, hives, sores and boils.These conditions are most often diagnosable by visual inspection. An important component of dermatological assessment is recognizing patterns, features, signs and symptoms. Using your computer camera through our platform, you can upload multiple photos safely and directly to your physician who can also store them to assess changes over time. The photos are transmitted with lossless compression giving your doctor a precise view of the tissue.

Diarrhea & Vomiting

Diagnosing the cause and determining appropriate treatment of diarrhea typically begins with very focused clinical questions. Your physician may recommend a special diet, over the counter and/or prescription medications, or follow-up care. Our doctor are often able to determine the cause and provide treatment for vomiting during a video consultation by reviewing your medical history, asking questions, and performing an exam.

Eye Conditions

Having an issue with one or both eyes can significantly and severely impact a patient’s lifestyle, causing not only pain but the inability to carry out necessary tasks. There are three basic categories: minor issues, moderate issues that require interim care followed by specialist care, and severe issues. Diagnosing the condition and severity often begins with questions about pain, discharge and previous eye damage, and the collection of a patient’s medical history. Then on camera, the physician can have the patient do several eye maneuvers and ask for still photographs to fully assess the eye.

Specialist Referrals

Our doctors can advise you on whether you need a specialist and the type of specialist you should see – saving you guesswork, time and money. It also provides you with privacy allowing you to get a specialists refferal you might feel uncomfortable asking your family doctor for.

Second Opinion

Our doctors can analyze another doctor's diagnosis through a video consultation with you and any test results adiministered by the other doctor so you feel confident about your treatment plan.

Travel Clinic

Travel is exciting: new places, new foods, new experiences. Unfortunately travel can also bring with it exposure to pathogens, germs and overall stress on your body. Our doctors can assist in all stages of travel. Whether you are about to travel, are currently traveling or have just returned from travel, ConnectMed video consultations are available wherever you have internet access, which can be very assuring!

Lab Results Analysis

Our doctors can analyze your lab results and explain what results mean and how you could take action to improve areas of concern.

Health Record Storage

ConnectMed offers you a free EHR (electronic medical record) that stores all of your personal health information in one secure place online. You can use this to keep your medical history up-to-date and share with your other health care providers.
